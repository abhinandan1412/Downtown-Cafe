Veg noodles are a popular dish made from boiled or stir-fried noodles combined with a variety of vegetables and often flavored with sauces or seasonings, creating a flavorful and nutritious meal.
Chhole Kulche ₹ 40
Chole kulche is a North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chole) served with soft and fluffy bread made from refined flour (kulcha), often accompanied by onions, green chilies, and chutneys.
White Sauce Pasta ₹ 110
White sauce pasta is a creamy Italian dish made by combining cooked pasta with a rich and velvety white sauce, usually consisting of butter, flour, milk, and cheese, resulting in a comforting and indulgent meal.
French Fries ₹ 80
French fries are a most popular side dish made from sliced and deep fried potatoes, usually seasoned with salt, and enjoyed for their crispy texture and also with savory flavor.
Chilli Potato ₹ 90
Chilli potatoes are a flavorful Indo-Chinese appetizer made by coating fried potato wedges in a spicy and tangy sauce, often garnished with onions, bell peppers, and spring onions.
Onion & Capsicum Pizza ₹ 120
Onion & capsicum pizza is a type of pizza topped with slices of onions & bell peppers, combined with other ingredients like cheese, tomato sauce, and additional toppings, creating a delicious and colorful flavor combination.
Masala Dosa ₹ 120
Masala dosa is a popular South Indian dish that consists of a thin and crispy fermented rice and lentil crepe, filled with a spiced potato mixture. It's often served with coconut chutney and sambar (a tangy lentil-based vegetable stew), making it a flavorful and satisfying meal.
Palak Paneer ₹ 130
Palak paneer is a North Indian dish made from fresh spinach (palak) and paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cooked together in a creamy and flavorful spiced gravy. It's a nutritious and popular vegetarian dish often served with rice or Indian bread such as naan or roti.
Kadhai Paneer ₹ 110
Kadhai Paneer is a North Indian dish where paneer (Indian cottage cheese) is cooked with a mix of onions, bell peppers, tomatoes, and aromatic spices in a "kadhai" or wok-style cooking. It's a flavorful and slightly spicy dish often enjoyed with Indian bread or rice.
Aloo Paratha ₹ 50
Aloo paratha is a popular Indian bread made by stuffing whole wheat dough with a spiced mixture of mashed potatoes (aloo). It's then rolled out and cooked on a griddle with ghee or oil until golden and crispy, often served with yogurt, pickles, or chutney.
Malai Kofta ₹ 135
Malai kofta is a rich and creamy North Indian dish where deep-fried vegetable or paneer (Indian cottage cheese) balls are served in a flavorful tomato-based gravy. The gravy is typically made with cream, nuts, and aromatic spices, creating a luxurious and indulgent dish often enjoyed with naan or rice.
Veg Mushroom ₹ 130
Veg include mushrooms as a primary ingredient in a vegetarian context. Mushrooms are often used in various cuisines to create flavorful and hearty dishes like mushroom stir-fry, mushroom risotto, mushroom pasta, and mushroom curry, among others.
Veg Biriyani ₹ 95
Veg biryani is a fragrant and flavorful Indian rice dish made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, aromatic spices, and herbs.
Special Thali ₹ 100
The Thali offers a diverse and balanced culinary experience, showcasing the rich flavors and textures of different Indian dishes in a single meal.
Cold Coffee ₹ 60
Cold coffee is a refreshing beverage made by blending or mixing chilled coffee with milk, sugar, and often ice. It's a popular drink, particularly during warmer weather, and can be customized with flavors like vanilla, chocolate, or caramel and topped with whipped cream or a dusting of cocoa powder.
Strawberry Shake ₹ 70
A strawberry shake is a sweet and creamy beverage made by blending fresh or frozen strawberries with milk, ice cream, or yogurt. It's a popular fruity treat enjoyed for its refreshing taste and smooth texture, often garnished with whipped cream or strawberry slices.
Hot Chocolate ₹ 50
Hot chocolate is a comforting drink made from melted chocolate or cocoa powder mixed with hot milk or water. Whipped cream, marshmallows, or a sprinkle of cocoa powder are common toppings for this warming beverage, especially during colder months.
Chocolate Shake ₹ 80
A chocolate shake is a creamy and indulgent beverage made by blending chocolate syrup, cocoa powder, or melted chocolate with milk and often ice cream. It results in a rich and sweet drink that's beloved for its chocolatey flavor and smooth consistency.
Oreo Shake ₹ 80
An Oreo shake is a delicious beverage made by blending Oreo cookies, usually along with milk and ice cream, to create a creamy and flavorful drink. The cookies add a distinctive chocolatey and cookies-and-cream taste, making it a popular treat enjoyed by many.
Vanilla Shake ₹ 80
A vanilla shake is a classic beverage made by blending vanilla-flavored ice cream with milk. It results in a smooth and creamy drink with a sweet and comforting vanilla taste. Vanilla shakes are versatile and can be customized with various toppings or additional flavors for a delightful treat.
Rasmalai ₹ 60
Rasmalai is a popular Indian dessert made from soft paneer dumplings that are soaked in a creamy and sweetened milk syrup infused with cardamom and saffron. It's a rich and indulgent treat often garnished with chopped nuts, making it a favorite on special occasions and celebrations.
Mojito ₹ 65
A mojito is a refreshing cocktail made with white rum, fresh mint leaves, lime juice, sugar, and soda water. It's a popular drink known for its zesty and minty flavor, often served over ice in a highball glass, making it a perfect choice for cooling off on a warm day.
